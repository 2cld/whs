Exit Hwy 218 on Hwy 78 travel east for six miles to where Hwy 78 "Ys" with Oasis Avenue. Veer left (north) onto OasisAvenue.
The first road right, at the grain elevators, is 130th Street. Turn right onto 130th Street and travel into the country. Once you have
crossed the bridge over Crooked Creek, the cemetery rests on a gentle rise on the north side of the road.
