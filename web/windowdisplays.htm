The first Monday of each month brings a new theme for the window displays at the Winfield Historical Society. Notice of the coming month's theme is published in the Winfield Beacon and posted in the window and all citizens are encouraged to contribute. Items loaned are retrieved the first Monday of the following month, which allows for the next month's display.

We deeply appreciate the loan of any item relating to the theme, and we doubly appreciate the donation of any item relating to Winfield. All donations will receive a receipt.
