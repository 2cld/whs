Greetings from Winfield, Iowa!

Thank you for visiting our web site. The Winfield Historical Society is a dedicated group of people striving to preserve Winfield history. As the world turns faster and lifestyles change, we believe it's important to save our rural heritage. We can all learn from the past and in so doing be inspired for the future.

We encourage all like-minded people to become members of our society. Your dues will help finance our efforts to record and treasure the local history that otherwise would quickly disappear. Any and all donations are welcome and tax deductible.

Thank you,
Judy Rawson, Founding President
