winfield scott township
cemetery














Exit Highway 218 onto Highway 78. Go east on Hwy 78 for six miles to the "Y" of Highway 78 and Oasis Avenue.
Turn (north) on Oasis. The first road right (east) is 130th Street. Turn right on 130th and travel into the countryside.
Once you have crossed the bridge over Crooked Creek, the cemetery is on a gentle rise on the north side of the road.
