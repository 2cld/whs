You are invited...

Writers, Mentors, Local History Buffs, Researchers, Students in Grades 6 through 12, Senior Citizens (the older the better), Thespians, Crooked Creek Days participants. WHAT? A Planning Meeting to brainstorm and discuss ideas, rules, and activities having to do with the 2008 Commodore's Writing Contest and Crooked Creek Days. WHEN? Tuesday, February 19th at 4:00 p.m. WHERE: The Winfield Public Library. WHY? The goal of the contest is to awaken participants to a love for reading, researching, and writing stories about local Winfield residents of the 1920s-1950s. (Monetary prizes will be awarded to the top three authors.) Anyone having a copy of "The Winfield Beacon/The First Hundred Years," please bring it with you.
